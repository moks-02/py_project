Our Mission

AccessToilet is dedicated to helping people with disabilities find accessible public toilets with ease. Our mission is to make the world more accessible by providing accurate, up-to-date information about toilet facilities that meet the needs of people with various disabilities.

How It Works

Find Your Location

Use your device's GPS or search for a specific location to find toilets near you.

Filter Results

Filter toilets based on your specific accessibility needs, such as wheelchair access or changing tables.

View Details

Get detailed information about each toilet, including accessibility features, opening hours, and user ratings.

Get Directions

Get step-by-step directions to your chosen toilet facility.

Our Data

Our toilet data comes from multiple sources, including:

  • Government databases of public facilities
  • Partnerships with businesses and organizations
  • User contributions and reports
  • Regular audits by our accessibility team

We strive to ensure all information is accurate and up-to-date, but conditions may change. If you find any inaccuracies, please use the "Report Issue" feature to let us know.
